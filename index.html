The Process
From Conversation to Care
Five intelligent steps that turn a natural chat into meaningful, personalised mental health support — in minutes.
Step 01
💬
Safe Conversation
Start a warm, judgement-free dialogue. No forms, no clinical jargon — just talking at your own pace.
Step 02
🧠
Deep Listening AI
Sentiment analysis, keyword mapping, and tone detection run quietly in the background as you speak.
Step 03
📊
Wellbeing Assessment
PHQ-9 and GAD-7 aligned scoring detects signals of anxiety, depression, loneliness, and burnout.
Step 04
🤝
Empathetic Response
The AI validates your experience and keeps you feeling heard and safe throughout the conversation.
Step 05
🎯
Smart Care Routing
Based on your needs, you're guided to self-care tools, verified therapists, or crisis services instantly.
